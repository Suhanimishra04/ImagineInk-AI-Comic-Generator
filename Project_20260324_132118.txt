A small town called Black Forest, once had an old lady and her daughter lived there. One day, the mother went to the market to sell some vegetables. The daughter stayed home alone and decided to go to the forest near her house for playing with her friends. The sun was shining brightly as she sat on the grass under the tree. Suddenly she saw something weird. She turned around and screamed loudly. Something was chasing her. She ran towards the house very fast. She entered the house safely and locked the door. The strange thing stopped following her at that moment. She heard a loud scream coming from outside the house. She immediately opened the door and found her mother lying dead on the floor. The demon was standing behind her mother’s body. It disappeared just like it came. The girl thought that she should tell about this incident to her father but she didn’t know where he is now.
