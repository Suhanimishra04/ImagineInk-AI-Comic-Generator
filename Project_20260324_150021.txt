The old tree was shocked at what he had done, but the man said he needed the wood for something. After hearing this, the old tree asked the man why he wasn’t taking care of the old tree instead of just cutting it down. The man told him that the tree was ugly and he didn’t want anyone looking at it. The old tree was hurt by what the man said and decided to tell the man how he felt. The man did not understand what the old tree was saying so he got mad at him. The old tree said that the man should be more careful with the trees around his house because some of them might fall on top of his head if he didn’t take care of them. The man then took his axe and chopped off a branch from the old tree without even asking first.

The old tree fell into a deep sleep after being cut down by the man.
